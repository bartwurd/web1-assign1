About Me

Hi, I'm Brennan Bova. I'm originally from Ottawa but thanks to prior schooling and work, I've also lived in Vancouver where I studied at the Vancouver Film School, and Halifax for a job at an animation studio. During my 8th year in Halifax I moved back home to Ottawa because I missed my home and the animation industry here was more vibrant. That was in 2019.

Within a year of me returning to Ottawa, the pandemic began and my work went remote. This ended up being a blessing in disguise because I was able to get a lot of design work from studios in Toronto that I couldn’t have worked for before remote work. Between 2020 and 2025 I worked for 5 different studios in Ontario and also created an animated short called Join which screened in festivals around the world. I came to Algonquin to increase my skills further and become a very well-rounded artist. I am excited to learn more about HTML and CSS.
